My Course Details

Here is a list of the courses I am currently taking and have completed during my studies.

Foundations of Information Technology

This subject introduces the basic concepts of computers and IT. You will learn about computer hardware, software, data storage, networks, and the internet. It helps students understand how technology supports daily life and business.

Modules covered

  1. Introduction to Computers and IT
  2. Computer Hardware and Software
  3. Operating Systems and Applications
  4. Data Storage and File Management
  5. Computer Networks and Internet Basics
  6. Cybersecurity and Digital Ethics

Mathematics for Computer Science

This subject teaches the mathematical concepts used in computer science. It builds logical and analytical thinking for solving technical problems. You'll learn topics like logic, sets, functions, and probability.

Modules covered

  1. Logic and Boolean Algebra
  2. Set Theory and Relations
  3. Functions and Graphs
  4. Matrices and Determinants
  5. Probability and Statistics
  6. Number Systems and Algorithms

Problem Solving & Programming Fundamentals

This course teaches students how to approach problems logically and solve them using programming. It covers the fundamentals of programming languages, writing algorithms, and implementing them with variables, loops, conditional statements, and basic data structures. Students also learn debugging and error-handling techniques to create efficient programs.

Modules Covered:

  1. Introduction to Programming and Algorithms
  2. Flowcharts and Pseudocode
  3. Variables, Data Types, and Operators
  4. Conditional Statements and Loops
  5. Arrays and Functions
  6. Debugging and Problem Solving Techniques

Database Management System (DBMS)

This course focuses on storing, retrieving, and managing data using databases. Students learn relational database concepts, SQL commands, normalization, designing database schemas, and managing transactions. It provides the foundation for backend development and handling real-world data efficiently.

Modules covered:

  1. Introduction to Databases and DBMS
  2. Data Models and ER Diagrams
  3. Relational Model and Normalization
  4. Structured Query Language (SQL)
  5. Database Design and Implementation
  6. Transaction Management and Security

Environmental Studies for Technology

This subject explains the relationship between technology and the environment. Students learn about sustainable development, pollution control, and how technology can help protect nature.

Modules covered:

  1. Introduction to Environment and Ecosystem
  2. Natural Resources and Biodiversity
  3. Environmental Pollution and Its Control
  4. Global Warming and Climate Change
  5. Sustainable Development and Green Technology
  6. Environmental Laws and Awareness

New Age Life Skills

This subject focuses on developing personal, emotional, and social skills. It helps students build confidence, teamwork, and communication for both academic and professional success.

Modules covered

  1. Self-Awareness and Emotional Intelligence
  2. Communication and Interpersonal Skills
  3. Time and Stress Management
  4. Decision Making and Problem Solving
  5. Teamwork and Leadership
  6. Digital Etiquette and Social Responsibility
